Project #6 Data Portrait -10/12/2021

*Data of My Working Time and Energy Level

Design Process

For this assignment, I spent a week tracking my schedule of when to start and end working in a day, a record of my energy level, and created a data-self portrait. In the graphic, the start and end points of the waves show the time frame, and the fluctuations demonstrate my energy level.

Data Tracking-

Reflection

Given by this assignment, I had the opportunity to record my working time and to visually see how it reflects my energy level on that day and the next day. As you could see in the graphic, a heavy work day on Thursday definitely affected my energy level and productivity on Friday. Being able to see the connection on the visualized data, it eased my panic of worrying about not doing enough and not being able to finish projects as soon as possible. It definitely reminded me of the importance of taking a break that helped me to reduce stress while working, and that overload work had really affected my performance and stress levels.
